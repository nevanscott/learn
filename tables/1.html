Let’s start off with a couple of tables marking up calendars in month view:

<h2>April 2007</h2>
<table>
<thead>
	<th>Su</th>
	<th>Mo</th>
	<th>Tu</th>
	<th>We</th>
	<th>Th</th>
	<th>Fr</th>

	<tr>
		<td>1</td>
		<td>2</td>
		<td>3</td>
		<td>4</td>

		<td>6</td>

	…

Notice the use of thead, th, and tbody. Along with being more semantic, these will give us more CSS ‘hooks’ to work with.
