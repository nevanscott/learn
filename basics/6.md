<p>The <code>text-align</code> property allows us to center the title and byline over the body of the essay, like so:</p>

<pre>
body {
	font-size: 11pt;
	line-height: 14pt;
	width: 330pt;
	margin-left: 100px;
	}
<ins>h1 {
	text-align: center;
	}
h2 {
	text-align: center;
	}</ins>
</pre>
